About us

Your Premier Apparel Partner

Welcome to ABC - Apparel Branding Company, where we transform your vision into exceptional branded apparel. With over 25 years of experience, we've established ourselves as a trusted leader in custom uniforms and corporate wear across India. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and skilled craftspeople work together to deliver premium quality garments that perfectly represent your brand identity. From schools and corporations to hospitals and hospitality, we provide comprehensive uniform solutions tailored to your unique needs. At ABC, we believe that every stitch tells your story, which is why we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation to create apparel that makes a lasting impression.

our history

Established - 1995

Founded in 1995, ABC has grown from a small family-run business to one of India's leading uniform manufacturers. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create high-quality, comfortable uniforms that organizations would be proud to wear. Over the decades, we've expanded our capabilities, invested in cutting-edge technology, and built a team of skilled professionals who share our passion for excellence. Today, we serve thousands of clients across India, from prestigious educational institutions to Fortune 500 companies. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has earned us numerous industry accolades and, more importantly, the trust of our clients. As we continue to grow, we remain true to our founding principles of craftsmanship, integrity, and personalized service.
